# Mortgage Repayment Simulator

A clean, interactive Streamlit app that goes beyond basic mortgage calculators.

This tool doesn’t just tell you your monthly payment — it shows how your loan behaves over time, especially when real life happens and payments get missed.

---

## What this does

- Calculate **monthly mortgage payments**
- Track **loan balance month by month**
- Simulate **missed payments**
- Visualize everything using **line and bar charts**

---

## Why this is different

Most calculators assume perfection.

This one doesn’t.

Miss a payment?  
The balance grows.  
Interest keeps ticking.  
And you see the real impact instantly.

---

## Features

- Monthly payment calculation using standard amortization formula  
- Full **amortization schedule**
- Select **specific months to skip payments**
- **Dynamic loan balance simulation**
- Visual insights:
  - Loan balance over time (line graph)
  - Principal vs interest breakdown (bar graph)
  - Actual payments per month
